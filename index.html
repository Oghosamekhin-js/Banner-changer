Rice: The Real MVP?

Rice is great, but it’s even better when turned into legendary dishes:
🔥 Jollof Rice – The spicy, tomato-packed West African icon that starts food wars.
🥢 Fried Rice – Stir-fried, soy sauce goodness, perfect for leftovers.
🧀 Risotto – The creamy Italian dish that tests your patience but delivers.
🌱 Rice & Beans – The timeless, protein-packed power couple.
But let’s be real—Jollof Rice reigns supreme. 🍛🔥
